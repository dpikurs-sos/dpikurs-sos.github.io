Work with Us

In return for commitment and dedication, SOS Kilkenny offers a unique working environment, excellent training and career opportunities and a supportive workplace. SOS Kilkenny promotes a culture of diversity and inclusion, learning and health and wellbeing.

Benefits of working with us include:

  • 24 days Annual Leave
  • Sick Pay Scheme
  • Maternity Leave
  • Pension Scheme
  • Employee Discounts
  • Employee Assistance Programme
  • Bike to Work Scheme
  • Study Assistance Scheme
  • Continual Professional Development Opportunities

For more information on working with us, please email recruitment@soskilkenny.com
