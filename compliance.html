Compliance

SOS Kilkenny demonstrates strong compliance with relevant regulatory bodies, adhering to national standards for disability services in Ireland. Our residential supports operate in accordance with guidelines set by the Health Information and Quality Authority (HIQA). Regular audits and inspections are conducted to ensure continuous quality improvement, safety, and the delivery of person-centred care highlighting our dedication to accountability, ethical practices, and the wellbeing of the individuals we support.

We also adhere to the regulations set by the Health and Safety Authority and financial regulators, including the Charities Regulatory Authority, the Revenue Commissioners and the Health Service Executive, as our main funders. This includes meeting health and safety standards to safeguard staff and the people we support, as well as complying with financial regulations to ensure transparency and accountability in financial management.
