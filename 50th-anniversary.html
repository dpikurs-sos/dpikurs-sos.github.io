50th Anniversary

Celebrating Five Decades of Supporting People in Kilkenny

A Legacy of Care and Support

For half a century, SOS Kilkenny has been at the heart of our community, providing essential supports to adults with intellectual disabilities and autism. What began as a small initiative has grown into a comprehensive service that touches the lives of hundreds of people across Kilkenny City and County.

Our journey has been marked by innovation, compassion, and an unwavering commitment to the people we support. From our early days to the present, we've remained true to our founding principles while adapting to meet the evolving needs of our community.

Celebrating Our Community

Our 50th anniversary celebrations brought together the entire SOS Kilkenny family - the people we support, our dedicated staff, families, friends, and community partners. It was a time to reflect on our achievements, honour those who have contributed to our success, and look forward to the future with optimism and determination.

The celebrations highlighted the incredible work being done every day across our various programmes and services, showcasing the talents, skills, and achievements of the people we support. It was a powerful reminder of why we do what we do and the positive impact we have on our community.

Watch the specially commissioned documentary, created by Pat Ryan, to mark our 50th Celebrations.

Looking Forward to the Future

As we celebrate this significant milestone, we're also looking ahead to the next 50 years. Our commitment to innovation, person-centred supports, and community engagement remains stronger than ever. We continue to evolve our services to meet the changing needs of the people we support and the wider community.

The future holds exciting opportunities for growth, learning, and development. We're committed to building on our strong foundation while embracing new approaches and technologies that will enhance the quality of life for the people we support. Together, we're building a more inclusive and supportive community for all.
