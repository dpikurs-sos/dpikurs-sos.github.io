Committed to creating a sustainable future for people and planet

Environmental Sustainability Ambition Statement

At SOS, we are dedicated to supporting people to live full and meaningful lives.

For us, true wellbeing means achieving a balance across physical, mental, emotional, social, and spiritual health so that every person we support can thrive in their own unique way.

We also understand that the wellbeing of people and the health of our planet are deeply connected. The planet must thrive so that people can thrive.

That's why we are committed to a holistic and sustainable approach in everything we do - one that places both people and the environment at the centre.

We will continue to create purposeful and environmentally conscious supports that ensure people reach their full potential and that contribute to a more sustainable future for all.

Sustainability Actions

  • We facilitate education programmes for the people we support and our staff team.

  • We have a Green Team who champion and drive sustainability initiatives across our organisation.

  • We prioritise Green Procurement sourcing products and services that have minimal environmental impact over their lifecycle.

  • We are running a Waste Reduction programme across the organisation.

  • We are actively seeking funding to improve the energy efficiency of our organisation.
